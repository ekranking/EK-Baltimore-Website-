Baltimore Treats is a website that gives tourist information about different cafes and restaurants in Baltimore, Maryland. We include dining facilities from bakeries to seafood. We will add more places as time comes. For now, here are the best places in Baltimore.

Koba ("Child of the Heavens" in Africa) Cafe is a charming, coffee spot in Fort Avenue. The coffee is brewed and serves breakfast and lunch. Menu includes sandwiches, wraps, and pastries. Koba Cafe is opened from 6am-6pm.

225 N Charles St, Baltimore, MD 21201 (443) 573-4620

Cafe Poupon is a French-themed bakery. It bakes and selles sweet treats, such as cookies and cakes of all types, including sheet and round cakes. They also create wedding cakes. Hard Rock Cafe

View Photo

View Photo

602 E Pratt St, Baltimore, MD 21224 (410) 522-2929

Located in the historic original Power Plant Building in the Inner Harbor, the Hard Rock Cafe Baltimore sits between the world-renowned National Aquarium in Baltimore and Harbor East, and is within walking distance of the Baltimore Convention Center, Oriole Park at Camden Yards and M&T Bank Stadium, along with many of Baltimore's other fun and historical attractions.

At the Hard Rock Cafe Baltimore, there is something for every Rock Star. Immerse yourself in rock history with authentic memorabilia from rock ‘n’ roll legends, all while enjoying mouth-watering American cuisine as well as signature made-to-order cocktails. Don’t forget to stop in our Rock Shop, which features limited-edition Hard Rock Cafe Baltimore merchandise.
